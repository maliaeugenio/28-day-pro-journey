User Actions
What the pro does
Downloads app from store, opens for first time
Enters name, trade category, contact info. Sees required steps
Uploads photos, writes bio, sets service area + budget. Often abandons here.
Receives re-engagement push/email. May return to complete profile
Hits 100% profile complete. Sets initial budget. App activates targeting
Receives push notification: "You have a new lead!"
Receives in-app tip on how leads work, what a good response looks like
Receives nudge to boost visibility by increasing budget or refining geo
Opens lead, reads job details, decides to respond or ignore. Key drop-off.
Lead charged to account. Pro sees deduction. Reacts to cost
Pro pauses, decreases budget, or contacts support with objections
Receives proactive outreach: ROI framing, budget guidance, optional call
Decides to stay active and continue OR goes dormant
Profile fully active, receiving regular lead flow. May upgrade budget
Reviews lead history, checks job conversion, adjusts services or coverage area
Changes geo targeting radius, service categories, budget. Explores advanced settings
Receives win-back sequence: free lead credit, discount, or check-in
Logs back in, re-engages with leads OR remains inactive
Yes → Active Pro path
No → Early dropout path
✅ Active, receiving charged leads, track toward LTV. Business relationship established.

Pain Points
Friction & drop-off risks
⚠️ Cold start anxiety — no social proof at entry
⚠️ Long form = low completion; no value preview shown
🔴 HIGH DROP-OFF: Profile abandonment before seeing any leads
Key Problem
No reward loop. Pro is doing all the work before seeing any value. No "what will this look like" preview.
⚠️ Re-engagement messages feel generic, not personalized
⚠️ Dead zone between completion and first lead. Trust erodes in silence.
⚠️ Lead quality unclear — pro doesn't know if it's a good lead
⚠️ Tips feel too generic, not tailored to trade or region
⚠️ Budget nudge feels like upsell, not helpful advice
🔴 HIGH DROP-OFF: Poor response rate. Pro doesn't know what to say or when.
Evidence
Lead acceptance rate below 90% avg. Lead-to-hire drop-off is significant.
🔴 HIGH DROP-OFF: Surprise charge without clear ROI context creates instant distrust
⚠️ No friction-reduction in place — pro pauses or leaves without intervention
⚠️ Support outreach may feel reactive and too late
⚠️ Decision moment — no clear signal to stay or go; no in-app trust reinforcement
No major new pain if pros reach here — primary goal is optimization
⚠️ Dashboard is complex for non-digital-native tradespeople
⚠️ Geo/budget settings are powerful but intimidating; no guided experience
⚠️ Win-back campaigns may arrive too late if churn decision is already made
⚠️ Last chance intervention — no clear escalation path
🔴 At this point, churned pros are very hard to recover
Minimal pain. Ongoing optimization opportunities (review collection, referrals, upsell to premium features)

Key AI Moments
Where AI changes the experience
🤖 Cold Start Classifier
Predict hot vs. cold start at signup using trade category + zip. Tailor onboarding path and lead preview accordingly.
🤖 Mock Lead Preview
Show curated sample leads for their specific trade + location during onboarding to demonstrate value before completion.
🤖 AI Profile Coach
Real-time scoring + suggestions: "Your bio is vague. Try: '[Trade] professional with 8 years in [City].'"
🤖 Re-engagement Personalization
LLM-crafted re-engagement message using pro's name, trade, city, and profile completion status.
🤖 Lead Volume Predictor
At profile completion, show: "Based on your trade + area, you can expect ~[X] leads/week at your current budget."
🤖 Lead Quality Scorer
AI evaluates each incoming lead against the pro's profile and scores: match quality, urgency, likelihood to convert.
🤖 Response Suggestion Engine
3 draft reply options tailored to the job type. Pro can tap-to-send or customize. Dramatically lowers response friction.
🤖 Budget ROI Model
Predict and visualize expected lead volume change from a budget increase. Framed as a business investment, not an upsell.
🤖 Time-to-response Alert
AI monitors response time and surfaces: "You're 3x more likely to win if you respond in the next 20 minutes."
🤖 Churn Risk Signal
Model detects charge + silence pattern. Triggers intervention before pro manually cancels. Suggests ROI framing.
🤖 Friction Reduction AI
When pro pauses, AI shows comparative data: "Plumbers in your zip who stayed active this week got [X] more leads."
🤖 Smart Support Routing
AI triages support requests and auto-inserts relevant FAQs, reducing human support load while increasing speed to answer.
🤖 D14 Progress Summary
Auto-generated plain-language summary: leads, responses, estimated pipeline value. Makes invisible value visible.
🤖 Business Health Score
Composite AI score (profile strength, response rate, budget efficiency, lead quality). Gamified + actionable.
🤖 Simplified Intelligence Layer
Plain-language summaries replace raw data tables. "You won 2 jobs last week. That's better than 65% of new pros."
🤖 AI Targeting Advisor
Natural language interface for geo + budget optimization. "Tell me what kind of jobs you want more of" → AI adjusts targeting.
🤖 Early Churn Prediction (D21)
Model trained on D14 signals predicts D28 churn risk. Triggers personalized win-back earlier than current flow.
